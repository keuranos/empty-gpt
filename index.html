⚡ Invoice Nudge Studio
Micro-SaaS idea · low-maintenance

Automated payment reminders for freelancers and agencies.

Invoice Nudge Studio is a lightweight web app that turns late invoices into on-time payments. Upload a client, set a due date, and send polite, branded reminders in one click.

€500–€1,000 Target monthly revenue
25–45 Paying clients at €19–€29
<1 hr/week Operational effort

Why it works

  • Reminders reduce late payments by 30–50% for freelancers.
  • Users plug in existing email + payment links.
  • No complex integrations needed for an MVP.
MVP focus: reminder templates + tracking dashboard. Upsell later with automated follow-ups.

Business model in a glance

Simple subscription pricing + upsell = predictable income with low maintenance.

Target niche

Freelancers, boutique studios, consultants, and small agencies billing €2k–€10k/month.

  • Feel payment delays immediately.
  • Need professional communication quickly.
  • Usually manage invoices alone.
